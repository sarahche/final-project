EnvisionVR

Scope - storyboarding, visual design, user research
Tools - InDesign, Illustrator
Roles - user researcher, visual designer
Team - Michelle Chen, Shirley Chen

The Jewish Association on Aging is a Pittsburgh-based senior care facility that aims to provide services and support members of the Pittsburgh community affected by dementia, Alzheimer's, and other memory problems; our goal was to ideate and explore the ways that we can enhance the lives of the JAA's residents. Then with our proposal, we designed a poster to describe and advertise our idea. We presented the final proposal and poster design to a number of JAA's staff members.

01 Research
My first visit to the JAA was a tour and deep-dive of the facilities and services that they offered. There are day services that members can attend while their family members are at work, hospice facilities for residents with physical difficulties, and long-term facilities for residents with cognitive impairments. For our resident interviews, we were careful to formulate simple and direct questions that would be easier to answer.
02 Topic
After gathering our research, we went through ways that we could encourage the residents to engage in different activities that would enrich their experience at the JAA. From personal observation and multiple staff and resident interviews over multiple trips to the JAA, we concluded that there was an emphasis on light and space. We wanted to focus on a way to give residents a sense of freedom that went beyond the JAA's walls.
03 Storyboard
With a desired experience in hand, we illustrated the step-by-step process that a user would take to enjoy VR, starting from its introduction to the JAA's residents to its usage. By illustrating each step in the process from beginning to end, we mapped out a user's entire experience.
04 Iteration
We explored a number of different color palettes we could utilize for our poster design. With a VR concept, we emphasized a dreamlike escape from reality. We wanted to make it clear to a viewer what the subject for the proposal was at first glance and to use the steps from the storyboard to illustrate the process of its use.
